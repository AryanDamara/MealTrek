Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Average delivery time is 30-40 minutes depending on location and restaurant busy hours. You'll receive real-time updates on your delivery status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay), UPI, and cash on delivery. All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, the order goes to the restaurant and cannot be cancelled.

What if my order arrives late?

If your order arrives late beyond the promised delivery time, you'll receive a refund of the delivery charges automatically.

How do I report an issue with my order?

You can report issues through the "Help" section in your account or contact our support team using any of the contact methods on this page.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer refunds for cancelled orders, late deliveries, or if your order doesn't meet our quality standards. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

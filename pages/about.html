About MealTrek

Bringing Delicious Food to Your Doorstep, One Order at a Time

Our Story

MealTrek was founded with a simple vision: to revolutionize the way people order food online. We started in 2023 with a passion for connecting hungry customers with their favorite restaurants and ensuring that every meal arrives fresh, hot, and on time.

What began as a small startup with just 10 partner restaurants has now grown to over 500 restaurants across multiple cities. Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and innovation has made us one of the most trusted food delivery platforms in the market.

Today, we serve thousands of happy customers every day, delivering more than 10,000 meals daily. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, and we're dedicated to making that experience seamless and enjoyable.
